Questions

Location FAQs

Do you charge a travel fee for locations outside Lethbridge? +
For locations within Lethbridge city limits there is no travel fee. For surrounding communities like Coaldale and Picture Butte, a small surcharge of $10–$20 may apply. For Taber and locations beyond 40km we assess case by case. We always confirm any travel fee before finalising your booking.
Can you detail my car at my workplace or a public parking lot? +
Yes — we service vehicles at homes, offices, apartment buildings, shopping centre parking lots, and most public spaces. We carry our own water and power supply so we don't require any hookups from your location. Just provide the address when booking.
What do I need to prepare before you arrive? +
Nothing at all. We bring everything — water, power, all products and equipment. Just ensure the vehicle is accessible (unlocked and parked somewhere we can work around it freely). We recommend removing any valuable personal items before we arrive as a courtesy to our team.
Do you service rural properties outside of town? +
Yes — we service farms, acreages, and rural properties within our general service radius. If you're outside the listed communities, give us a call with your postal code or address and we'll confirm availability and any applicable travel fee right away.
Do you operate in winter? +
Yes, we operate year-round. Interior detailing can be done in any weather since we work inside the vehicle. Exterior wash services are weather-dependent — we may need to reschedule in extreme cold or heavy precipitation. We communicate proactively if conditions might affect your appointment.
