Got Questions?

Pricing FAQs

Are the listed prices final? +
The prices shown are starting prices. The final quote may vary based on your vehicle's size (sedan vs SUV vs truck), its current condition, and any add-ons you select. We always confirm your final price before beginning any work — zero surprise charges at the end.
Do you charge extra for larger vehicles? +
Yes — larger vehicles require more time and product. As a guide: sedans are at the base price, SUVs and minivans add approximately $20–$40, and full-size trucks or vans may add $30–$60 depending on the package. We confirm this during the booking process.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, Interac e-transfer, and all major credit cards. Payment is collected on completion of the service once you've inspected the result and are satisfied. We never request upfront payment.
What is your cancellation policy? +
We ask for at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a $25 late fee. We understand things happen — just call us as soon as you know and we'll do our best to work with you.
Is there a satisfaction guarantee? +
Absolutely. If you're not completely satisfied with the result, contact us within 24 hours and we will return to address any concerns free of charge. We stand behind every job — no debate, no hassle.
Can I get a discount for multiple vehicles? +
Yes — we offer fleet and multi-vehicle discounts for businesses or households booking two or more vehicles at the same time or location. Contact us directly to discuss a custom rate.
How do specialised service quotes work? +
Ceramic coating and paint protection film pricing is based on a physical inspection of your vehicle. We assess paint condition, size, and required coverage before providing a written quote. The inspection is completely free with no obligation to proceed.
