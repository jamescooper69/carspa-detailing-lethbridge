Our Story

Built on a Passion for Perfectly Clean Cars

Lethbridge Car Spa was born from a simple frustration: why should getting a professional car detail require rearranging your entire day? We started as a single-van operation in Lethbridge, showing up at driveways and office parking lots with everything needed to deliver a spotless result on-site.

Word spread quickly. What began as a local side project grew into a full-time business as Lethbridge discovered that mobile detailing could be more thorough, more personal, and more convenient than anything a fixed shop could offer. We invested in better equipment, better products, and rigorous training for every team member.

Today, Lethbridge Car Spa serves hundreds of vehicles each year. We've earned our reputation one detail at a time, and we work every day to maintain the standard that got us here: exceptional results, every vehicle, every time.

Our Mission

To deliver the most thorough, convenient, and satisfying mobile detailing experience in Lethbridge — treating every vehicle as if it were our own, and every customer as if they were a lifelong friend. We exist to raise the standard of what professional auto care looks like in our community.
