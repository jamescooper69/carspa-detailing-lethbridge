What's Included in Every Detail

These eight core treatments form the backbone of every Lethbridge Car Spa service.

Deep Vacuuming

Multi-stage vacuum passes through every seat, carpet, and crevice.

Surface Sanitize

All hard interior surfaces wiped down and sanitized top to bottom.

Steam Disinfection

High-temperature steam kills bacteria and allergens without chemicals.

Mat Deep Clean
